Derive the change to be made

The things that we notice are :

1] There are many columns that are not relevant to our questions and hence, they wont be playing any role in our insights calculations.

Solution : We can delete the columns that are not important to our insights.

2] There are many columns in the budget and the revenue section that have 0 value.This is critical as we wont actually know the budget of the movie or the total revenue earned by the movie.The profit trend calculated on the basis of these 0' values would lead to wrong insights and may deviate the trends.

Solution : Dropping the rows where the budget or revenue is 0 is the only solution in order to avoid wrong calculations.

3] There are null values encountered in production companies column.

Solution : We could think of filling the mean values in the vacant place but the production companies cannot be filled with any values randomly as it may affect our calculations.We would drop these rows as there are no significant amount of rows wit null calues.

4] The data must also be inspected for duplicates if any.

Solution : There are hardly any duplicates in the data

Conclusions

Following Conclusions can be derived from the above analysis : Q 1] For a movie to be successful and profitable, The revenue plays a very important role in getting the desired results. Although the Vote Ratings do not play a vital role in the success of a movie. The movie making business is more profitable in the recent years as compared to the earlier decades.

Q 2] A movie would have better chances of succeeding if it has one of the top 10 casts as derived in the question 2 analysis.

Q 3] The top 10 grossing movies are included in the analysis and can be further studied in order to predict the characteristics of a successful movie.

Q 4] The movie making business has had a huge growth in the last 2 decades and hence, the movie makers need to be more watchful for their competition as compared to the previous decades.

Q 5] The most profitable genres are derived in the analysis and it may be the basis for genre selection for the movie makers.

Q 6] The runtime of all the successful genres can be mapped to the average runtime and the movies can be edited around that average time.
